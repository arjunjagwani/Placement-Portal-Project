Adani

Company Overview

The Adani Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate, focusing on sectors like energy, logistics, agribusiness, and more, with a vision to build a nation.

Selection Process

  • Online Aptitude Test
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Exam Pattern

The Adani hiring process includes:

  • Aptitude Test: Numerical Ability, Logical Reasoning, and English
  • Technical Test: Domain knowledge and practical problem-solving
  • Behavioral Assessment: Teamwork and leadership scenario-based questions

Additional Details

Adani Group values leadership and innovative thinking. Focus on showcasing problem-solving and interpersonal skills during interviews.
