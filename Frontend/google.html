Google

Company Overview

Google is a multinational technology company specializing in internet-related services, such as search engines, advertising, and software solutions. Known for its innovation and employee-friendly culture, Google ranks among the top employers globally.

Selection Process

  • Online Coding Challenge
  • Technical Interviews (3-5 Rounds)
  • Googleyness & Leadership Interview

Exam Pattern

The Google hiring process involves:

  • Online Challenge: Algorithmic problems and coding tasks
  • Technical Rounds: Data Structures, Algorithms, and System Design
  • Behavioral Round: Evaluating cultural fit and leadership qualities

Additional Details

Google looks for problem-solving aptitude, teamwork, and innovation. Prepare with a focus on algorithms and real-world system design challenges.
