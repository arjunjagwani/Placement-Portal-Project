Capgemini is a global leader in consulting, technology, and outsourcing services. It works with clients to create and deliver business and technology solutions tailored to their needs.

Selection Process

Exam Pattern

The Capgemini assessment includes:

  • Aptitude Section: Logical, Verbal, and Numerical questions
  • Technical Section: Coding, Debugging, and Pseudo-code
  • Communication Skills: English proficiency test

Additional Details

Strong communication skills and teamwork capabilities are valued in candidates. Preparation for group discussions is essential.
