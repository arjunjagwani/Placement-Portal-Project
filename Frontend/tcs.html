Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

Company Overview

TCS is a global leader in IT services, consulting, and business solutions, with a large network of innovation and delivery centers. TCS is known for its commitment to helping clients solve complex business challenges using innovative technology.

Selection Process

  • Online Assessment
  • Technical Interview
  • Managerial Interview
  • HR Interview

Exam Pattern

The TCS exam pattern includes the following sections:

  • Quantitative Aptitude: Mathematical questions to test problem-solving abilities
  • Logical Reasoning: Logical and analytical reasoning skills
  • Verbal Ability: English language skills
  • Programming Logic: Questions on programming concepts
  • Coding: Coding questions to test problem-solving and programming skills

Additional Details

The TCS recruitment process focuses on technical knowledge and problem-solving ability. Practice coding in multiple languages and strengthen your fundamentals for success in their process.
