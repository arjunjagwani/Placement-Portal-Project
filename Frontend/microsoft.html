Microsoft

Company Overview

Microsoft is a global technology leader known for its software, services, devices, and solutions that help people and businesses realize their full potential. Its best-known products include the Windows operating system, Microsoft Office, and Azure cloud services.

Selection Process

  • Online Coding Test
  • Technical Rounds (2-3 Interviews)
  • HR Interview

Exam Pattern

The Microsoft hiring process involves the following sections:

  • Online Test: Coding, Data Structures, and Algorithms
  • Technical Rounds: Problem-solving, System Design, and Debugging
  • Behavioral Round: Leadership principles and culture fit

Additional Details

Microsoft emphasizes hands-on coding skills and expects candidates to be proficient in their chosen programming languages. System design knowledge is also crucial for higher-level positions.
